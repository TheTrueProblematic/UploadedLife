Age-Gating with ID Verification

Collecting government IDs to gate online experiences can reduce harm, but it also concentrates risk. Companies suddenly hold the keys to someone’s most sensitive documents.

Pros: Platforms can keep truly adult experiences separate, brands get clearer analytics, and regulators feel confident the rules are being followed.

Cons: Centralizing IDs multiplies the fallout of a breach, increases surveillance, and often locks out people who either lack documents or do not trust the storage practices.

Alex’s story shows how a requirement meant to “keep everyone safe” can spill over into real-world debt, stress, and erosion of autonomy when the wrong database leaks.
